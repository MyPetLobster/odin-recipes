Peanut Butter and Butter Sandwich

To make a perfect peanut butter and butter sandwich all you need is:

  1. Two slices of bread
  2. Your favorite peanut butter
  3. Butter

Toast your bread to a medium brown. When the bread is HOT, add copious amounts of butter so that it seeps deep into that bread. Now spread peanut butter to your liking and chow down.
