Boiled Eggs

The perfect hardboiled eggs are surprisingly easy to make. Simply fill a pot with water, then add the uncooked eggs BEFORE you even turn the heat on. Then turn the heat on high and wait for the water to reach a boil. Once the water is boiling, turn off the heat and put a lid on the pot. Keep the pot on the same heating element. Set a time for 18 minutes. After 18 minutes, put the eggs into a bowl of ice water. There you have it!
